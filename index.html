Our Business Partner: Olfactory Lab

Olfactory Lab is a growing business that specializes in creating custom fragrances for individuals and businesses. They have a dedicated customer support team that handles inquiries, issues, and requests from customers regarding their products and services.

Project Plan

Our group project focuses on creating a full-stack help desk ticketing system for Olfactory Lab. This system will streamline customer support processes, allowing customers to submit tickets, track their status, and receive timely responses from the support team.

The help desk ticketing system will include the following features:

  • User-friendly interface for submitting and managing tickets
  • Automated email notifications for ticket updates
  • Admin dashboard for support team to manage tickets
  • Reporting and analytics to track ticket resolution times and customer satisfaction
  • Integration with Olfactory Lab's existing systems

Our goal is to enhance the customer support experience for Olfactory Lab, ensuring that customers receive prompt and efficient assistance with their inquiries and issues.
